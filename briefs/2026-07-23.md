## Industry Buzz

**IPA Bellwether Q2 2026: brand-building spend outpaces activation**  ·  `MUST-KNOW`
- **Source:** [LBB Online — "Bellwether Q2 Reactions Show Brands Need to Be Planning for the Long-Term"](https://lbbonline.com/news/bellwether-report-q2-reactions), Jul 16, 2026
- **What:** UK marketing budgets revised up to a net balance of +6.9% (second-highest in two years), but the composition shifted toward the long game: video hit a seven-quarter high, PR and events grew, and shorter-term online-activation spend was cut for the first time in seven quarters. Market research spend fell for a sixth straight quarter.
- **Why us:** This is hard third-party data confirming our own "don't hoard cultural capital, compound it" argument — brands are visibly choosing patient, sustained presence over spend spikes. A ready-made proof point for the Q2 Digital Culture Review's thesis, and for Think Forward.
